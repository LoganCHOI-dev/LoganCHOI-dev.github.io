Privacy Policy

Knight's Tour : Extreme Puzzle

https://LoganCHOI-dev.github.io/Knightstour/privacy-policy.html

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Knight's Tour : Extreme Puzzle handles information. The app is a mobile puzzle game based on the knight movement rule from chess. The app does not require account creation, sign-in, a subscription, or user-submitted personal profile information.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app stores gameplay progress and app settings locally on your device. This may include unlocked board progress, completed board state, saved puzzle sessions, haptics settings, and sound settings.

This local game data is stored using Android local storage and is not uploaded to a server operated by the app developer. You can remove this local data by clearing the app data in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob through the Google Mobile Ads SDK to show ads, including banner ads and interstitial ads. Google and its partners may collect or receive information from the app and your device to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Depending on your device settings and Google's SDK behavior, this may include device identifiers such as the Advertising ID, app interactions with ads, diagnostic information, performance information, and approximate location derived from network or device signals.

You can learn more about Google's advertising data practices at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Analytics And Firebase

The app currently does not use Firebase Analytics or Firebase Crashlytics. If Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, or another Firebase service is added in a future version, this policy will be updated before that version is published.

Planned analytics, if added later, should be limited to non-identifying app events such as game start, board selection, puzzle completion, hint usage, ad display state, crash diagnostics, and performance diagnostics.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • The app does not create user accounts.
  • The app does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, calendar, messages, payment information, or precise location.
  • The app does not access your photos, videos, camera, microphone, or files.
  • The app does not provide online rankings, social features, chat, or user-generated content.

Data Sharing

The app developer does not sell your personal information. Information processed by Google AdMob may be shared with Google and its advertising partners as described by Google's policies and SDK disclosures.

Children's Privacy

The app is a general puzzle game and is not directed primarily to children. If the app's target audience or Google Play age settings change in the future, this policy and the app's data practices will be reviewed again.

Security

The app uses platform and SDK-provided network communication for ads and related services. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but the app is designed to avoid collecting personal account information from users.

Your Choices

  • You can clear local game progress by clearing app data in Android settings or uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage the Android Advertising ID and ad personalization options in your device settings.
  • You can contact the developer for privacy questions or deletion requests related to app-managed data.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated when the app changes, including when production AdMob IDs, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, or other third-party SDKs are added. The effective date above will be updated when the policy changes.
